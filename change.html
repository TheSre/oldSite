This page has resources related to current issues in the world and potential ways to work towards positive change on those fronts.

  • Effective Altruism is a movement aimed at using rigorous methods to identify the most effective ways to give our time and money. One of its core claims is that even ordinary people can have impressively large positive impacts on the world if they focus on the most effective approaches.

  • ACLU Petitions: The ACLU has a number of petitions on important issues that are very easy to sign.
